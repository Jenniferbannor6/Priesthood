Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Bible College

What are the admission requirements?

We require a genuine calling to ministry, a letter of recommendation from your pastor, and a willingness to learn. No prior theological education is necessary. Applicants must be at least 18 years old.

How long is the program and when does it start?

The program is 6 months intensive. We have two cohorts per year: January and July. Classes are held on Saturdays (12-5 PM) and Tuesdays/Thursdays (6-8 PM).

Can I pay in installments?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans. Contact Rev Paul at 0249392178 to discuss installment options. We want to make ministry training accessible to all called by God.

Is the certificate recognized internationally?

Yes, our ordination certificate and ministerial license are recognized internationally for ministry work. Many of our graduates are serving in Ghana, other African countries, Europe, and America.

What support is available after graduation?

Graduates receive lifetime access to our alumni network, ongoing mentorship, ministry placement support, and opportunities for continued education. We also provide recommendation letters for ministry positions.
